## Getting Started

1. **Quick Start:** Read the [README](README.md) for a 2-minute overview
2. **Learn the Philosophy:** Review [About](docs/about.md) to understand why Rabit exists
3. **Implementation Guide:** Check out [Implementation Guide](docs/guide.md) for terminology and best practices
4. **Full Specification:** Dive into [Specification](docs/rabit-spec.md) for complete technical details
5. **See Examples:** Explore [Examples](examples/) for real-world burrow structures
6. **Code It:** Use the [Client](packages/rabit-client/) to traverse burrows programmatically
7. **Integrate with AI:** Try the [MCP Plugin](packages/rabit-mcp/) for Claude and other AI tools
